How it works

From brief to feedback
in minutes, not days.

1
Upload your brief
Paste or upload your assignment instructions. AI reads it and generates a milestone plan — one card per section of your report.
2
Invite your team
Add teammates by their 4.0 username or share an invite link. Assign sections so everyone knows exactly what they own.
3
Submit drafts
Each teammate pastes or uploads their section draft. No digging through group chats or 12 versions of the same file.
4
Get AI feedback
Hit Analyze. AI checks each section against your rubric, flags what's missing, and gives a completion score — before you submit.
